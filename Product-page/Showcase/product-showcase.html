Rasdium - crowdfunding foundation

Rasdium, also known as R.Fund, is a crowdfunding foundation that supports innovative projects in the fields of science, technology, art and social impact. Rasdium aims to empower creators and visionaries who want to make a positive difference in the world. By connecting them with a global community of backers, Rasdium helps them raise funds, gain feedback and build networks. Rasdium is more than just a platform, it is a movement for change.
